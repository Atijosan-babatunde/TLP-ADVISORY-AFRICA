Legal Practice

We provide legal advice to startups and scaleup whether they are tech companies, tech-enabled or innovation-driven enterprises. We also advise SMEs and entrepreneurs in general.

A few of the packages we have designed based on our client needs include:

Founders Advisory Package:

This is for yoy if you are just kicking off your business. We will advise you on what you need to have in place to start a business, govern your founders relationship and provide you with the relevant legal documents.

Fundraising Advisory Service:

If you are raising funds to scale your business, whether debit or equity, this is for you. We will provide you with legal support from term sheet negotiation to the signing of the investment documents.

Company Secretarial Service:

If all you need is to help you with your corporate meetings and filings, we have a tailor-suited packeage for you as well.

Data Protection and Advisory:

We advise on how best to commercially the data you collect without violating privacy provisions. We also support you with drafting and privacy policies, cookie policies and carrying out data audits.

Outside General Counsel:

This is our comprehensive all- purpose retainer package. You will not belive we are not seated with you in your office.

Transaction Advisory Package:

If all you need is end-to-end support on a specific transaction, we will support you as well, from attending meetings, structuring transactions, negotiations to drafting substantive agreements.
